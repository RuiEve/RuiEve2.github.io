About Me

What Makes Our Photography Special?

Hello! I am Blanca Nieves, a passionate photographer and mother of two daughters. My love for photography is not only based on capturing moments, but on immortalizing the emotions, stories and memories that make each person and each family unique. With years of experience and knowledge in photographic techniques, I strive to offer professional work that reflects the beauty of the most special moments in your life.

My approach is close and personalized, understanding that each session is special. Whether it's family portraits, weddings, maternity sessions, or milestone events, I make sure each image captures the essence of who they are and what they represent. For me, photography is not just a job; It is my passion, and that is reflected in the dedication and creativity that I put into each project.

I invite you to choose me not only for my experience, but also for my commitment to offering you authentic photos full of life. Together we will create memories that will last forever. ;I'm ready to be part of your story through my lens!
